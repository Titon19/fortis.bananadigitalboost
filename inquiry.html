Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

FAQ

Do I need to be an existing customer to submit an inquiry?

No. We welcome inquiries from new and existing clients. If this is your first engagement with Fortis, we'll use the inquiry as the starting point for our commercial relationship.

FAQ

How quickly will I receive a quote after submitting?

For standard products with known availability, we typically respond within 1 business day. For international sourcing or custom specifications, allow 2–3 business days for a preliminary quote.

FAQ

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product category. For MRO and PPE supplies, there is generally no minimum. For bulk procurement or consignment arrangements, contact us to discuss volume thresholds.
